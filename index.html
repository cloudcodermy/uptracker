Pricing

Each order fulfillment costs less than $0.01

  • How does the pricing works?

    To fulfill orders, you need to purchase UpTracker credits. Each credit can be used to fulfill one order and each credit cost $0.01 or less.

  • How does UpTracker updates the fulfillments of orders?

    UpTracker will read from a file to identify the orders to fulfill. Tracking number and company will be updated to each product based on the SKU that you have mapped in the field mapping page.

  • What are the supported formats for Uptracker to fulfill my orders?

    The supported formats are CSV, XML, XLS and XLSX for now.
